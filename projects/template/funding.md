# Funding and Donations

### Local Sources
- City or county sustainability funds
- Local businesses or foundations
- Crowdfunding campaigns

### Ongoing Sustainability
- Membership or subscription model
- Monthly donation drives
- Volunteer time contributions

### Transparency
Encourage publishing simple income/expense reports.

### Value-Sharing
If your project receives donations, consider donating a small share (e.g. 5%) to the **Commons Library** to keep the ecosystem maintained and accessible to all.
