# Communication Guide

### Story
Write a simple story that anyone can tell about your project.
> “We created [Project Name] because our community needed a way to [purpose].”

### Channels
List your main communication channels (Facebook, flyers, website, events, etc.)

### Tone
Describe how you want to sound (friendly, local, empowering, hopeful, transparent).

### Outreach
Ideas for reaching the right people (partners, volunteers, funders).

### Visuals
Optional: logo, color scheme, or imagery that represents your project’s spirit.
