## üîÑ 4. Peer-to-Peer (Experimental)

The **Commons Library** is developing a way for local Thingeries to publish and sync their inventory **peer-to-peer**,  
using **Hypercore** or **DAT** ‚Äî no servers, no cloud costs, and total data ownership.

- **Why it matters:** helps communities collaborate without dependence on central platforms.  
- **How to prepare:**  
  - Keep your data structured (CSV or JSON)  
  - When P2P sync is ready, you‚Äôll be able to share your inventory directly with other Thingeries.
