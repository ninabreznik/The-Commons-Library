# Setup Guide

1. **Find a Space**
   - Could be a library, community center, or a donated garage.
   - Needs some shelving and a secure entry system.

2. **Gather Tools and Items**
   - Start with donated items in good condition.
   - Categorize: tools, kitchen gear, electronics, etc.

3. **Create Inventory**
   - Use a shared spreadsheet or open-source tool like **Lend Engine** or **Inventory app** built on your peer-to-peer stack (Hypercore / DAT).

4. **Membership System**
   - Register members with small annual fee.
   - Keep a simple sign-up and borrowing process.

5. **Volunteer Team**
   - Recruit locals to handle inventory, onboarding, maintenance.

6. **Open Day**
   - Launch event, invite press, show the idea of shared economy in action.
