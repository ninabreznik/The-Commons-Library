# Onboarding

### For Volunteers
- Host an introduction meeting once a month.
- Pair new volunteers with experienced members.
- Offer clear roles: inventory manager, communications, events, etc.

### For Members
- Simple membership form and safety policy.
- Short borrowing tutorial.
- Encourage them to donate items or time.

### Tip
Celebrate milestones: first 100 members, 100 items borrowed, etc.
